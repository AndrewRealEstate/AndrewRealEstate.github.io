City of Carrollton

The City of Carrollton does not fit comfortably into any universally understood category. It’s a small town with amenities and assets typically seen in much larger urban areas. Much of Carrollton’s unique character can be attributed to this independence from the Atlanta job market. In fact, Carrollton’s position as a regional economic hub causes the daytime population of the city to increase to 40,016. - City of Carrollton Comprehensive Plan
